Wasselni Application Overview

A comprehensive look at Wasalni, a smart delivery solution.

**Wasalni** is a smart and efficient delivery app that connects users with nearby drivers for fast and reliable transportation of goods and parcels. Users can place delivery requests, track orders in real time, and pay on delivery or through digital methods.

Development & Technologies:

The app was developed using **Flutter** for a consistent cross-platform experience on both Android and iOS, and powered by a **Laravel** and **Firebase** backend for real-time notifications, user management, and secure data handling. **Google Maps** integration allows for accurate location tracking and route optimization.

With a clean user interface and dedicated dashboards for drivers and store owners, Wasalni offers a seamless delivery experience for both senders and couriers.

Key Features:
  • User and driver registration
  • Order creation and assignment
  • Real-time tracking with maps
  • Instant push notifications
  • Ratings and reviews system
  • Multiple payment options (cash on delivery, digital)
  • Multilingual support
