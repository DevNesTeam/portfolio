Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about working with DevNest and building your mobile app.

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

Development time varies depending on the complexity of the app, but most projects take between 4 to 10 weeks from start to launch.

Do you only build Android apps or also iOS?

We use Flutter, a cross-platform framework, which allows us to build apps for both Android and iOS from a single codebase.

Can you help me publish the app on Google Play or App Store?

Yes, we offer complete publishing support including preparing assets, writing store descriptions, and managing the submission process.

Do I need to have designs before I contact you?

No worries if you don’t — our UI/UX team can design your app from scratch based on your brand and target audience.

What technologies do you use for backend development?

We use Laravel, a powerful PHP framework, to build scalable, secure, and performant APIs for your mobile applications.

How much does it cost to build an app with DevNest?

Pricing depends on the features and complexity of your app. We offer flexible packages for startups and businesses — contact us for a free quote.

What kind of support do you offer after the app is delivered?

We provide 6 months of free maintenance after delivery. This includes bug fixes, minor updates, and ensuring your app runs smoothly on new OS versions.

Can I update the app later with new features?

Yes! We build apps with scalability in mind, so you can always add new features or improvements in future versions.

Do you sign NDAs to protect my app idea?

Absolutely. We’re happy to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement to keep your app idea secure and confidential.

Who owns the code after the app is built?

You do. Once the project is complete and payment is made, you will have full ownership of the source code and all deliverables.

Can you redesign or upgrade an existing app?

Yes, we can analyze your current app and offer UI/UX improvements, performance upgrades, or complete rebuilds as needed.

What’s your development process like?

We follow a clear workflow: discovery → design → development → testing → launch → support. You’ll be involved in every key step.
